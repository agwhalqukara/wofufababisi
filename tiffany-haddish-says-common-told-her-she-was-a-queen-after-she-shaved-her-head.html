Tiffany Haddish Says Common Told Her She Was A Queen After She Shaved Her Head

September 30, 2024
blog

According to Tiffany Haddish, whether or not people appreciate her newly bald head doesn’t matter much to her, because she loves it. As fans of the Night School star know, she recently cut all of her hair off while on Instagram Live, and many have wondered out loud why she would do such a thing.

Haddish said it was ultimately the right decision, however, and even her boyfriend, Common, approved of it. You can see what Tiffany had to say about the situation in the YouTube video below.

Regarding her romance, Tiffany claimed it’s the best one she has had in her life so far. The actress says it’s the first time she has felt like she didn’t have to limit herself to make a man feel better about himself.

As for how Common feels about her bald head, Tiffany claimed he loves it. The actress says she was overwhelmed by his compliments and also feels less stressed about her hair now that it’s all gone.

In recent news, Tiffany was in the headlines after she admitted to hanging out with the Toronto rapper, Drake. The star explained how she and Drake had actually planned to go on a dinner date together, but it never came to fruition.

According to Tiffany, in 2018, she and Drake worked together on the music video for “Nice For What,” however, the friendship started out on the wrong foot because he had sent her a bunch of messages that she didn’t even know about until he told her about them.

While at a party, the actress explained, Drake told her he had slid into her direct messages to try and get her into a music video. Drake wanted to take her for dinner but ultimately ran into a “family emergency” which put a stop to his plans at the last minute.
