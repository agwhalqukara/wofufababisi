Shana Goldberg-Meehan Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Shana Goldberg-Meehan is an American television producer and television writer.She was an executive producer of Friends and its spinoff Joey, which she also co-created along with Scott Silveri, whom she married in 2006. In 2005, she left Joey while Silveri stayed on the show. In 2010, she created the series Better with You, which ended in 2011.Goldberg-Meehan's father was comedy writer Gary David Goldberg, best known for creating Family Ties. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
