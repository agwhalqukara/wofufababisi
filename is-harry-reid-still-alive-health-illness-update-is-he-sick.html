Is Harry Reid Sick? Health And Illness Update

Talking about his illness update, Harry Reid was definitely sick and was not in a good health condition.

However, he is recovering and has won the tough fight against cancer.

He underwent pancreatic cancer surgery at Johns Hopkins Cancer Center on May 14, 2018, after a tumor was discovered on his pancreas during a routine checkup.

Following that, Reid was restricted to a desk at home and unable to move without the assistance of a walker, according to a January 2019 interview with The New York Times.

Reid revealed on February 25, 2019, that his cancer is in remission as a result of early identification and chemotherapy.
