Biography

DJ Yella was born in Compton, CA on December 11, 1967. Music producer and DJ best known for co-producing all three albums by legendary Southern California gangster rap crew NWA as well as Eazy-E‘s debut album Eazy-Duz-It. He later became a successful director and producer of films. He had a long and successful musical partnership with NWA’s Dr. Dre. He DJ’d in local Los Angeles clubs as a teenager and produced one of West Coast hip-hop’s earliest releases, World Class Wreckin’ Cru’s World Class, in 1985.
