Conor Maynard Biography And Net Worth

Conor Paul Maynard is a British singer, songwriter, YouTuber, and actor born and raised in Brighton, England. Conor Maynard was born on 21 November 1992. Like many other celebrities, Conor discovered his love and passion for music at a very tender age but had his breakthrough in 2012 when he won the MTVs Brand New 2012 award. Conor Maynard is signed to Warner Music Group and has been with the giant music firm since 2011.

While with Warner Music, Conor released his first album, “Contrast” in July 2012, a year after signing with the music group. Although a megastar, Conor Maynard manages to live a private life, however, it is public knowledge that he schooled at the Cardinal Newman Catholic School. Conor Maynard has two siblings; Anna and Jack. Jack and Anna are both prolific YouTubers with larges viewership and subscribers.
